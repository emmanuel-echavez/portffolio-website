About Me



Hi, I'm Emmanuel! I am an entry-level full-stack developer with a strong foundation in computer science, holding a bachelor's degree from Stockton University and an associate's degree in Computer Science and Game Development from Ocean County College.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained extensive experience in full-stack development, allowing me to build and optimize dynamic web applications. In addition to my technical background, I have been actively involved in Esports, serving as both a player and manager for Stockton University's Esports program. I have also worked with the Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC) as a league organizer, organizing hundreds of teams and thousands of players, contributing to the growth and structure of collegiate Esports competitions.

Recently, I have been making the transition into a career in IT and have obtained the CompTIA A+ certification and I am working towards the CompTIA Network+ certification. My diverse experience in software development, IT, and Esports management equips me with a unique skill set that allows me to adapt to various technical and leadership roles. I am eager to leverage my skills and passion for technology to make a meaningful impact in the industry.
